What We Do

Intelligent scheduling for complex manufacturing

Axiom is a production planning platform built for manufacturers who can't afford surprises. It maps your full process chain, surfaces bottlenecks before they become missed deadlines, and helps you reschedule in minutes — not hours.

Chain Visibility

See every step in your production process, with live utilization data and dependency tracking across all cells and resources.

Gap Detection

Axiom identifies slack, conflicts, and constraint clusters before they cascade — so you can act on a warning, not a miss.

Rapid Iteration

Model rush orders, machine outages, and priority shifts in real time. Compare scenarios and commit to a plan with confidence.
